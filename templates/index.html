Types of Insurance

Vehicle Insurance

Vehicle insurance protects your car or bike against unexpected damages, accidents, theft, fire, and natural disasters. It also provides third-party liability coverage required by law. With comprehensive vehicle insurance, you can drive confidently knowing that repair costs and liabilities are financially protected.

  • Accident & collision coverage
  • Theft and fire protection
  • Third-party liability protection
  • Cashless repair network support

Life Insurance

Life insurance ensures the financial security of your loved ones in the event of unforeseen circumstances. It provides a guaranteed payout to your family, helping them manage daily expenses, education costs, and future financial goals even in your absence.

  • Financial security for family
  • Long-term savings and investment options
  • Tax benefits under applicable laws
  • Flexible premium payment plans

Health Insurance

Health insurance covers medical expenses such as hospitalization, surgeries, diagnostic tests, and treatments. It helps reduce the financial burden during medical emergencies while ensuring you receive quality healthcare services when needed.

  • Hospitalization & surgery coverage
  • Pre & post hospitalization benefits
  • Emergency medical services
  • Cashless treatment at network hospitals

Home Insurance

Home insurance protects your house and belongings from risks such as fire, theft, natural disasters, and accidental damages. It ensures your property and valuables remain financially secure in case of unexpected events.

  • Protection against fire & disasters
  • Coverage for household valuables
  • Burglary and theft protection
  • Structural damage coverage

Business Insurance

Business insurance helps safeguard your company from financial losses caused by accidents, property damage, legal liabilities, and operational interruptions. It ensures business continuity and protects both assets and employees.

  • Property and asset protection
  • Employee coverage options
  • Public liability protection
  • Business interruption coverage

Property Insurance

Property insurance protects commercial and residential properties against damage caused by fire, storms, floods, vandalism, and other unexpected events. It ensures that property owners can recover quickly without heavy financial losses.

  • Fire and natural disaster protection
  • Coverage for buildings and assets
  • Repair and reconstruction support
  • Financial risk protection
